Introduction

Kosmo Gulf Trading Est. is a Saudi-based Trading Company specializing in electrical, mechanical, and hardware products for oil and gas and construction projects.

We support contractors and project owners by sourcing reliable, industry-standard materials that meet demanding technical and schedule requirements.

Driven by a customer-focused approach, Kosmo Gulf Trading Est. delivers quality products, competitive pricing, and responsive service to help clients complete their projects efficiently.
